Job Overview

We're looking for someone as excited as we are about removing opacity from the world of startups and private companies. At Radicle, we are looking to help investors* in startups make better decisions by building a community and platform that captures the conversation - insights, opinions, research and analysis - around these companies. We think important decisions deserve more than what is available today: simple ‘facts and figures.’

*We believe anyone who spends time with a startup is an investor - whether you invest your time as a partner, your career as an employee, or your dollars as a professional investor.

We’re looking for an experienced engineer who wants to put their mark on the world.

As one of the first members of the engineering team, you’re passionate about building products and working within a talented cross-functional team to do that. You have previously built frontend and backend applications that are indicative of your ability to rise to, meet and beat challenges.

Prior experience & professional qualities

  • Strong software engineering skills with the ability to build, deploy, and maintain a large, scalable codebase
  • Proficient with Python, Javascript, SQL, AWS, Git, CircleCI
  • Familiar with a JS framework like React and a web framework like Django
  • Experience with RESTful API design and integration
  • Rigorous attention to detail and testing for both function and performance
  • Obsession with clear, proactive and effective communication
  • Self-starter
  • Wants to build something
  • Is fun
