Director of Business Development

New York | Sales & Marketing | Full-Time

Job Overview

We're looking for someone as excited as we are about removing opacity from the world of startups and private companies. At Radicle, we are looking to help investors* in startups make better decisions by building a community and platform that captures the conversation - insights, opinions, research and analysis - around these companies. We think important decisions deserve more than what is available today: simple ‘facts and figures.’

*We believe anyone who spends time with a startup is an investor - whether you invest your time as a partner, your career as an employee, or your dollars as a professional investor.

As Director of Business Development, you will design, develop and execute the strategy to form critical partnerships across several categories which will be fundamental to our success.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Strategy Development
  • Product Development Input
  • Messaging and Approach
  • Deal Operations
  • Partner Success

Prior experience & professional qualities

  • At least 3 years' experience in a BD or Sales role at an operating company
  • Rigorous attention to detail, strong analytical capabilities and significant intellectual horsepower
  • High EQ, a quick-on-your-feet creative thinker and the ability to 'click' with anyone
  • A tenacious spirit marked by persistence, dogged determination and calm indifference to rejection
  • Obsession with clear, proactive and effective communication
  • Scrappy self-starter
  • Is fun
